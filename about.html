  • About Me

    My name is Justin Cook and am currently a third year student in the Mechatronics Engineering Program at McMaster University in Hamilton, Ontario. I grew up in Brampton, Ontario and, from the age of 4, had a keen interest in robotics; starting to use kits to make my own robotics from when I was 6. My interest in robotics lead me to decide to pursue Mechatronics Engineering as the degree closest to my desire career in building, coding, and designing modern robots and so in high school at North Park Secondary School I took Computer Engineering and Computer Science courses. Now as I continue my studies I edge ever closer to realizing my dream of working in the field of robotics.
